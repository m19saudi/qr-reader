# 📱 QR & Barcode Scanner

Experience full QR code and barcode scanning **directly in your browser**, with a clean and modern interface.
[LINK](https://m19saudi.github.io/qr-reader/)
---

## 🧠 What This Page Does

- ✔ **Camera Scanning**  
  Open your device camera and scan QR codes and barcodes in real-time.  

- ✔ **Image File Scanning**  
  Drag-and-drop an image or select a file from your device to decode QR codes instantly.  

- ✔ **Works Offline**  
  No external dependencies — everything runs locally in your browser.  

- ✔ **Live Preview & Modern UI**  
  Enjoy a sleek interface inspired by [scanapp.org](https://scanapp.org) demo style, with live camera preview and smooth interactions.  
  https://github.com/mebjas/html5-qrcode
---

## 🎨 Features & Highlights

- Clean and minimal design  
- Responsive layout for mobile and desktop  
- Instant results with clear visual feedback  
- Secure — your data never leaves your device  

---

## ⚡ How to Use

1. **Camera Mode**: Click "Open Camera", point at the QR code, and see the result instantly.  
2. **File Mode**: Drag an image onto the drop area or click to select a file.  
3. **Offline**: Close your internet connection — scanning still works perfectly.
